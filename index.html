MiG-21PD

Soviet Experimental VTOL Fighter

MiG-21PD in Flight

The MiG-21PD was an experimental Soviet aircraft developed in the 1960s. It was based on the MiG-21 and used additional lift engines to improve short takeoff and landing performance.

Specifications

  • Country: Soviet Union
  • Role: Experimental interceptor
  • Engines: 1 main jet engine + 2 lift engines
  • First flight: 1966
  • Status: Prototype only
